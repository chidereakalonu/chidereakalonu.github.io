Recipe #1

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: 6-8
Ingredients Quantity
Milk 3/4 cups
Eggs 2
Flour 4 cups

Step 1: Mix the milk and the flour using a mixer, manuel or electric.
Step 2: Stir the eggs in a separate bowl until it gets foamy: a merengue if you will :)
Step 3: Mix the merengue in with the other stuff.
Step 1: Pour the mixture into a well greased non-stick pan and put into the oven at 450 deg for 25 min.

Recipe #2

  • Time: 40 min
  • Serves: 2-3
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 4 cups
Milk 2 cups
Eggs 4

Step 1:Mix all ingredients together in one giant bowl. Just do it.
Step 2: Pour into a well greased non-stick pan.
Step 3:Put pan in oven at 375 deg for 15-17 min and now you're all done! :)
